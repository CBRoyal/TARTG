How to Prepare for The Amazing Race

For all of you Amazing Race fans, as you know right now for 24 years, The Amazing Race has been traveling to 6 continents, 97 countires, and traveling more than 1 million miles with 11-14 teams of two racing around the world for $1 million. Now you may think when you're watching it for the comfort of your own home, you may think the show will be easy to get through, but I can promise you it's not. Shows like this take a couple or a few years of preparation to really take on these certain elements of the show. You need to be able to have the physicality and mentality for all the challenges The Amazing Race will throw at you; you need to be able to handle the stress and mentally of international traveling and be able to book travel tickets properly; and you need to pack with you the necessary items you need for the show. Well, this website will show you all the necessary guidelines you'll need to follow to prepare yourself for this race adventure around the world.
